# Faceless YouTube Channel Starter Pack

## Channel Position

Build the channel around one clear promise:

> I am building an AI trading bot in public, paper trading only, and showing what actually works, what fails, and what the data says.

This keeps the channel focused while still allowing variety across trading systems, charts, crypto, psychology, and tools.

## Core Audience

- Retail traders who want proof-based trading content instead of hype.
- Beginner algo traders who want to learn journaling, backtesting, and automation.
- AI tool builders interested in trading dashboards, signals, and risk controls.
- Crypto and stock traders who like transparent paper-trading experiments.

## Content Pillars

1. AI Trading Bot Build-In-Public
2. Paper Trading Results and Audits
3. Chart and Setup Breakdowns
4. Crypto Paper Trading Track
5. Trading Psychology and Discipline
6. Trading Tools, Journals, and Automation

## Upload Rhythm

Recommended starter cadence:

- Monday: AI trading bot build/update
- Wednesday: chart/setup breakdown or paper-trade audit
- Friday: psychology, lessons learned, or tool walkthrough

Daily uploads can work later, but the first 30 videos should train YouTube around a consistent identity.

## Required Disclaimer

Use this in the video intro, description, and pinned comment:

> This video is for education only. I am paper trading and not giving financial advice. Trading involves risk. Do your own research.

## Production Stack

- Screen recording: OBS Studio
- Editing: CapCut or DaVinci Resolve
- AI voice: ElevenLabs or Murf
- Thumbnails: Canva or Photoshop
- Charts: TradingView screen capture
- Proof visuals: PMO dashboard, journal CSV, audit reports, score-band panels

## First Flagship Video

Start with:

> I Built an AI Trading Bot, But I Am Not Letting It Trade Live Yet

This explains PMO, builds trust, and sets the ethical tone: paper trading first, proof before live.
